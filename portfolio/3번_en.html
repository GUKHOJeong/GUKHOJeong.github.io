2. Challenge (Situation & Task)

üßê Situation

We planned an app to provide both 'fun' and 'training' for people who find it difficult to make facial expressions. The key was to accurately classify Korean facial characteristics and limited emotions (Joy, Anger, Surprise, Sadness).

üöß Difficulties

  • Dataset Limitations: Lack of Korean facial datasets and difficulty in capturing subtle expression changes.
  • Generalization Issue: While performance was high on the training set, detection rates dropped significantly in real camera tests depending on lighting or angles (Overfitting).

3. Process & Action

Although ViT had the best performance, its parameter count was too high for mobile. Thus, EfficientNet was selected as the final base model.

üîß Troubleshooting: Ensuring Generalization

  1. Model Complexity Up: Increased model capacity from EfficientNet B0 to B3 to learn finer features.
  2. Data Augmentation: Simulated various real-world environments through rotation, brightness adjustment, and noise addition.
  3. Analysis: Confirmed stabilization of Validation Loss in B3 model after applying augmentation.
