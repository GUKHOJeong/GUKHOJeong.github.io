2. Challenge (Situation & Task)

üßê Service Goal

Modern people are exposed to countless news articles but struggle to grasp the key context. We planned a service that collects the latest news on specific keywords in real-time, summarizes the content into 3 lines, and groups them by major topics.

üöß Key Difficulties

  • Duplicate News Flood: Due to breaking news competition, 3-4 identical articles are produced by different media outlets, degrading analysis quality.
  • Summary Model Consistency: Early models like KoBERT lost context or generated grammatically awkward summaries for long sentences.
  • Latency: The heavy natural language processing (Summary + Topic Modeling) caused long user wait times.

3. Process & Action

üß† NLP Pipeline Construction

  1. Model Selection (KoBERT vs T5):
    - KoBERT: Strong in processing long inputs but unstable in generation results.
    - T5-Base (Selected): Selected for showing the most natural Korean generation capability through Prompt-based ("Summarize: ...") fine-tuning.
  2. Topic Modeling (LDA): Applied Scikit-learn's LDA to automatically classify news by topic after extracting nouns with Mecab morphological analyzer.

üîß Troubleshooting: Data Quality & UX

  • Removing Duplicates: Filtered out 'copy-paste' articles with over 80% content similarity by calculating TF-IDF based Cosine Similarity within media groups.
  • Real-time Feedback (UX): Considering long analysis times, introduced `Flask-SocketIO` to visualizes progress (e.g., "Collecting data...", "Running summary model...") with a progress bar, reducing bounce rates.
